Entering grades with Quick Entry

Quick Entry allows you to input grades quickly from your desktop, mobile phone, or tablet.

To enter a grade:

  1. Select a subject.
  2. Select an assignment.
    The total possible points will appear next to the grade entry field.
  3. Select a student.
  4. Enter a grade.
    5. upGrade shows you the student's grade compared to the class's average grade.
  5. Optionally, enter a comment.
  6. Review the data, then select SAVE.
